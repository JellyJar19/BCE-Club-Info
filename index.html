Who are we?

Touch me again and I'll make sure you know who I am.

We are a group of students in Nanyang Technological University taking Business and Computer Engineering as a Double Degree, passionate to guide current and prospective students in their journey within NTU BCE. This is currently a passion project held up by 2 students who have too little free time and not enough bandwidth to do anything.

The NTU business and computer engineering double degree program aims to provide students with a well-rounded education in the disciplines of both Business and Computer Engineering.
