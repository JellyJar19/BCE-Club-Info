BCE Club Main Committee Recruitment

The Business and Computer Engineering club is looking for a main committee to help with events, sponsors and engagement opportunities! We are looking for passionate individuals who are keen to give back to the community and enhance students' university experience. Sign up now!

Roles and Responsibilities

These are the roles that we are looking for, and their repsective responsibilities.

  • Honorary General Secretary
  • Business Relations Manager
  • Financial Controller
  • Welfare Director
  • Publications and Publicity Director

Honorary General Secretary

The roles and responsibilities of the Honorary General Secretary are as follows:

  • Basic bookkeeping for the club.
  • Maintain a register of club members.
  • Take minutes at the club's Annual General Meetings.
  • Support the club President and Vice President as required.
  • Ensure that the club complies with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

Sign Up!

Business Relations Manager

The roles and responsibilities of the Business Relations Manager are as follows:

  • Engage with Businesses to organise engagement events
  • Source for sponsorships.
  • Work with the Financial Controller in the event of canvassing or other forms of fundraising.

Sign Up!

Financial Controller

The roles and responsibilities of the Financial Controller are as follows:

  • Keep an active record of all transactions and club funds.
  • Be in charge of bookkeeping in compliance with annual audit checks.
  • Collect and disburse funds on behalf of the BCE Club

Sign Up!

Welfare Director

The roles and responsibilities of the Welfare Director are as follows:

  • Chief Safety Officer at all events.
  • Work with the other portfolios to plan and execute events.
  • Be in charge of welfare events throughout the academic year.

Sign Up!

Publications and Publicity Director

The roles and responsibilities of the Publications and Publicity Director are as follows:

  • Be in charge of the club's social media presence.
  • Ensure that the club complies with the Copyright Act.
  • Take photos at the various events and come up wiht publicity for the various events!
  • Work with the Vice President for the maintenance of the BCE club website.
